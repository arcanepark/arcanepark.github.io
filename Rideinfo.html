RIDES AND ATTRACTIONS!






THE VIKING

If you are down to experience an adrenaline rush come out to ARCANE PARK to ride THE VIKING.

With intense swinging and crazy fun this ride will surely never disappoint your expectations of this ride.

Be a daredevil and sit on the edge of either side to experience the most!





THE DOOM OF PYTHON

Buckle up and get ready to experience a ride like never before.

The Doom of python does GRAVITY DEFYING maneuvers which you will never be able to experience anywhere else!

Hurry up and make your way to Arcane Park!





THE FLYING SAUCER

Ever wonder what it would feel like to ride in a UFO?

Come ride THE FLYING SAUCER to have a simulated experience of flying inside a UFO.

This is Singapore’s first spinning ride so don’t miss out on this one of a kind ride!





THE SONIC

Ever wanted to have a FREE-FALL experience?

SONIC gives you exactly just that.

A vertical drop for 9 floors which is followed by a series of left and right turns will make you want to go again and again and never leave!

To all those adrenaline freaks, come to ARCANE PARK soon to get an adrenaline rush like never before!





THE TSUNAMI POOL

Most tsunamis are only seen on TV’s but are never experienced.

Well we are about to change that. THE TSUNAMI POOL simulates the waves caused by a tsunami for you to experience it first hand.

There will be a series of 6-7 feet tall waves for 90 seconds once every 10 minutes so that you can experience it for how many times you would like to! Only at ARCANE PARK!





THE PHARAOH

To all those who have always wanted to experience a unique water slide instead of the usual boring ones, we have got you covered!

THE PHARAOH is a 800 feet water slide which extends underwater too for you to experience!

Say no more to all those boring water slide experiences and make your way to ARCANE PARK to raise your standards of a water slide!
